PETS
    DOG CAT FISH

The Dog

Dogs are loyal to its owner and show inconditional love. They are playful and like company, having at least two dogs is recomended if living in an apartment. For one dog having ample spaces would be ideal, like a big house or a yard

The Cat

Cats are independent, showing conditional love, if they are given what they want, they maight show some affection in return. They are curios, and also playful as dogs, but they don't require ample spaces. They do well indoors. If they live in a house, the roof will be part of their dominon.

The Fish

Fish are independent pets, they don't require much attention. They are quiet and calming to watch. They do well in small spaces, like apartments. A tank with a filter and a heater is ideal for tropical fish. They can be kept alone or in groups depending on the species.
